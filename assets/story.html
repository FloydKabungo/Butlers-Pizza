Every time a Butler's Pizza leaves our ovens, it carries with it a little piece of history. Behind the combination of herbs, sauce and fresh dough that makes our pizza so uniquely tasty, lies an original recipe created by one of South Africa's premier culinary personalities, Lesley Faull, the founder of SA's oldest and finest cookery school, Silwood Kitchen.

In 1989, one of Lesley's grandsons, Robert Wilkinson, decided to start a specialist pizza delivery outlet in Rondebosch. He approached his grandmother Lesley and his uncle Tim Faull for assistance, and before long the original Butler's recipe was born. Years later, Lesley would be proud to know that Butler's is firmly established as Cape Town's No.1 Pizza!
